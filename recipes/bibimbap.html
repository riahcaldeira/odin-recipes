Bibimbap!

Make a Delicious Bibimbap

Bibimbap is one of the most well known Korean dishes. A rice bowl topped with all sorts of seasoned saut√©ed vegetables, marinated meat (usually beef), a fried egg sunny side up, finished with a sprinkle of sesame and generous dollop of a sweet-spicy-savoury Bibimbap sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups cooked white rice
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tsp sesame seeds

Korean Beef & Marinade

  • 250 beef tenderloin
  • 1/4 green apple
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp honey
  • 2 tsp sesame oil

Vegetables

  • 2 carrots
  • 2 zucchini
  • 1 bunch of spinach
  • 8 dried shiitake mushrooms
  • 2 tsp garlic
  • 1/4 tsp fish sauce
  • 1.5 tsp soy sauce

Bibimbap Sauce

  • 4 tbsp gochujang paste
  • 2 tbsp mirin
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1.5 tsp soy sauce
  • 3 tsp white sugar
  • 1 garlic clove
  • 2.5 tsp sesame oil

Steps

Bibimbap Sauce

  1. Mix ingredients until sugar is dissolved

Marinated Beef

  1. Mix the marinade in a bowl, then add beef. Marinate for 30 minutes to overnight.
  2. Heat 2 tsp oil in a large skillet over high heat. Let excess marinade drip off then add beef. Cook for 3 - 4 minutes until cooked and there's some caramelised bits, then remove from skillet.

Cook Vegetables

  1. Shiitake: Heat 2 tsp oil oil in a skillet over medium high heat. Cook mushrooms for 2 minutes. Add 1.5 tsp soy, 1/4 tsp sugar, 1/2 tsp of garlic. Stir for 1 minute, then remove.
  2. Carrot: Add 2 tsp oil into the skillet, cook carrot until just tender (5 to 8 minutes), then remove.
  3. Zucchini: Cook as with carrot for 4 minutes.
  4. Spinach: Heat 2 tsp veg with a splash of sesame oil. Saute until starting to wilt. Add 1/2 tsp garlic, and salt to taste, stir, then remove. When cool, squeeze to drain out excess liquid.

Assemble

  1. Fry eggs in a skillet to your taste.
  2. Place warm rice in bowls.
  3. Top with vegetables and beef.
  4. Sprinkle with sesame seeds, drizzled with sesame oil. Serve with Bibimbap Sauce!
